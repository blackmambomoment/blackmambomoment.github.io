If you have other commitments during the summer but still want to enrich your learning, part-time summer programs offer a flexible alternative. Many summer programs in Chicago offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate students’ individual needs and preferences. Choosing the Right Schedule and Duration#

When selecting a summer program, carefully consider your schedule and availability to ensure that the program aligns with your plans. If you have other commitments, such as work or travel, a part-time program may be a better fit. If you thrive in a structured environment, a full-time program may be a good choice. If you prefer a more flexible and self-paced learning environment, a part-time program may be a better fit.

Scheduling Accommodations#
