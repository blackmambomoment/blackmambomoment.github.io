Popular destinations for fully funded summer programs include the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and many European countries. Here are a few examples of programs to consider:

  • The Global Leadership Adventures Program (GLAP): This program provides high school students with the opportunity to explore different cultures, learn leadership skills, and engage in community service projects in various countries around the world.
  • The American Councils Summer Language Institutes (SLIs): These institutes offer intensive language courses in Arabic, Chinese, French, German, Hebrew, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish, and Turkish. SLIs are located in various cities across the United States.
  • The International Summer School (ISS): The ISS offers a wide range of academic courses, cultural excursions, and social activities to international students from over 30 countries. The program is held at the University of California, Berkeley.
