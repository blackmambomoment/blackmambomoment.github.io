FAQ#

Q1: What are the eligibility criteria for Spotify student accounts?#

A1: To be eligible for a Spotify student account, you must be enrolled in an accredited college or university and have a valid student email address. Additionally, you must be at least 18 years of age and not currently subscribed to a Spotify premium account.

Q2: How can I verify my student status with Spotify?#

A2: To verify your student status, you will need to provide Spotify with a valid student email address. Spotify will then send you an email with a verification link. Once you click on the link, your student status will be confirmed, and you will be able to subscribe to a Spotify student account.

Q3: What is the cost of a Spotify student account?#

A3: The monthly cost of a Spotify student account is typically 50% less than that of a regular premium account. However, the exact cost may vary depending on your location.

Q4: How long does my Spotify student account last?#

A4: A Spotify student account typically lasts for 12 months. However, you may be able to extend your subscription for another 12 months by providing proof of your continued student status.

Q5: What happens when my Spotify student account expires?#

A5: When your Spotify student account expires, you will lose access to the student discount. You will still be able to use Spotify, but you will be charged the regular premium rate.
