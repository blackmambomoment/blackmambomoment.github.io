Amount of NSFAS Funding UNISA Students Can Expect#

When it comes to NSFAS funding, UNISA students can typically expect to receive assistance in the form of a bursary, which covers tuition, a lodging allowance, a meal allowance, a personal care allowance, and a learning material allowance. The amount of NSFAS funding UNISA students can expect depends on a few factors, including their family’s income, their academic performance, and the cost of their studies.

  • Tuition and Registration Fees: NSFAS covers all tuition and registration fees for students at UNISA. UNISA students will be required to pay for any additional fees, such as graduation fees and late registration fees.
  • Living Allowance: UNISA students receive a monthly living allowance to cover their living expenses, such as rent, food, and transportation. The amount of the living allowance is determined by the student’s family’s income and the cost of living in the area where the student lives.
  • Travel Allowance: UNISA students who live more than 400 kilometers away from their campus receive a travel allowance to cover the cost of transportation to and from school. The amount of the travel allowance is determined by the distance between the student’s home and the campus.
  • Other Allowances: UNISA students may also receive other allowances, such as a laptop allowance, a book allowance, and a personal care allowance. The amount of these allowances is determined by the student’s financial need.

Key Takeaways:

  • The amount of NSFAS funding UNISA students can expect depends on several factors, including their academic merit, financial need, and the estimated cost of study.
  • NSFAS funding covers tuition and registration fees, as well as a portion of UNISA students’ living expenses, such as food, monthly rent, personal care products, and transportation.
  • To apply for NSFAS funding, UNISA students must meet certain eligibility criteria, including South African citizenship, a household income below a certain threshold, and academic merit.
  • The NSFAS application process is typically open from September 1 to November 30 of each year.
  • NSFAS funding is awarded based on a points system, where students must accumulate a predetermined number of points based on their academic history, financial need, and personal circumstances.

Other forms of financial assistance available to UNISA students#

  1. Bursaries: These are typically awarded based on academic merit, financial need or a combination of both. Bursaries are usually offered by private companies, organizations or individuals.

Student loans#
