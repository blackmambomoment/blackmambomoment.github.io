NSFAS Accommodation Stipends 2024: What’s Covered?#

NSFAS recognizes the importance of affordable accommodation for students pursuing higher education. In 2024, the scheme will provide funding for accommodation costs up to a maximum of R48,000 per year. This amount is intended to cover the rental or bond payments, utilities, and basic amenities essential for comfortable living. Additionally, NSFAS offers a living allowance that can be utilized for expenses such as groceries, toiletries, and transportation. The living allowance varies depending on the student’s circumstances and the institution they’re attending.

Eligibility Criteria for NSFAS Accommodation Funding#

Securing NSFAS funding for accommodation requires meeting specific eligibility criteria. These criteria are designed to ensure that the funds are allocated fairly and reach those who need them most. To qualify for NSFAS accommodation funding in 2024, you must:

  • Be a South African citizen or permanent resident.

  • Be registered for full-time study at a public university or TVET college.

  • Demonstrate financial need through a rigorous means test.

  • Meet the academic requirements set by NSFAS.

  • Not be in possession of another qualification at the same level or higher than the one you’re applying for.

Application Process for NSFAS Accommodation Funding#

Accommodation Funding Details

  • NSFAS offers a fixed amount of R4,500 per year for accommodation, irrespective of the institution or location.
  • This amount is subject to change based on NSFAS’s annual review and budget allocation decisions.
  • The funds are disbursed directly to the accredited accommodation provider, streamlining the process for students and eliminating any administrative delays.

NSFAS Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must be South African citizens or permanent residents with a valid ID document.
  • Students should be enrolled full-time at a public university or TVET college in South Africa.
  • Applicants’ household income should be below the threshold set by NSFAS, ensuring that funding is primarily directed to those who genuinely need financial assistance.
  • Academic performance is considered, with students required to maintain satisfactory academic progress to remain eligible for funding.

Application Process

  1. Visit the NSFAS website and create an account.
  2. Complete the online application form, accurately providing all the required personal and academic information.
  3. Submit the required supporting documents, such as proof of income, academic transcripts, and a certified copy of your ID document.
  4. Submit the completed application and supporting documents before the stipulated deadline.
  5. Await the assessment of your application, which may take several weeks.
  6. Once approved, the funds will be disbursed directly to your accredited accommodation provider.

Terms and Conditions of Funding
