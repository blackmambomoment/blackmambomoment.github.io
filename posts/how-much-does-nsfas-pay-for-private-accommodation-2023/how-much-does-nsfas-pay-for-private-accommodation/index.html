Application Process:#

  1. Complete the Online Application:

Once you have completed the application, you must submit it online. You will need to attach the following supporting documents:

  1. Receive Your Financial Aid:

NSFAS will disburse your financial aid in installments. You will receive the first installment at the beginning of the academic year. The remaining installments will be disbursed at the beginning of each semester.
